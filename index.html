First Approach EA/ Worksite Wellness, LLC was developed by Chief Operating Officer, James Jenkins’ desire to enable positive change in the lives of employed military veterans and their families along with small businesses.

At First Approach EA we believe worksite wellness is the best way to engage employees, veterans alike to improve his or her quality of life. We offer a wide range of services such as Telemedicine Health, Preventive Educational Wellness Products, First Approach EA/ Worksite Wellness Coaching, and much more. All fees for services are PMPE.
